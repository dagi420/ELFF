Our Services

We offer a range of services to help you live more ethically and sustainably.

Filter coffee

We brew our filter coffee using the pour-over method, which brings out the best flavor and aroma of the beans. You can choose from different roasts and origins, and enjoy a smooth and balanced cup of coffee.

Espresso-based Drinks

We have a wide range of espresso drinks, from classic cappuccinos and lattes, to specialty drinks like mochas, macchiatos, and flat whites. You can customize your drink with different flavors, milks, and sweeteners.

Food

We serve breakfast, lunch, and snacks, all made with fresh and organic ingredients. You can find pastries, muffins, croissants, and scones, as well as sandwiches, salads, soups, and wraps. We have vegan, gluten-free, and dairy-free options as well.
